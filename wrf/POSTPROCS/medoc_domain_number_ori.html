MEDOC Retrieval

Source: gs-c1

Domain: 3


Instructions

  • Green, blue and red blocks indicate available final-fdda, preliminary-fdda and forecast data, gray blocks indicate missing data.
  • Light green, light blue, light red and light gray blocks indicate selected times.
  • < and > step backward and forward 1 hour, respectively.
  • << and >> step backward and forward 24 hours.
  • Select data by clicking on the desired times, then click the Download button to save the data.
  • The applet will automatically refresh the available data every 60 seconds. Turn off the auto-update feature if you want to browse through older data.
